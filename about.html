About Us...

The GK Institute of Commerce is a brand name which is pioneer in classroom coaching for classes 11th, 12th, CA-Foundation (formerly known as CA-CPT) and CA-Intermediate (formerly known as CA-IPCC). The Institute, established in March 2012, has so far won confidence, love and trust of over 3000 satisfied and successful students. The mission is simple: To revolutionize commerce coaching in the region and help students in carving a path which leads to triumph. Apart from focusing on qualifying exams, we also prepare students to cope up with practical situations that he may face in his student life, in his article-ship period and in future as a Chartered Accountant or any other professional responsibility. The GK team worked very hard with full dedication, devotion and enthusiasm. They were determined to provide the best education.
