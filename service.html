10+1 COMMERCE

The commerce stream is altogether a different world as students entering into it hardly have any knowledge about the subjects or what commerce is all about. Hence, class 10+1 is a very crucial and an important academic class. It is the class in which the foundation is laid down and concepts are introduced, which are tested throughout your professional studies. GK Institute of Commerce ensures proper conceptual clarity and builds a proper and firm base. Students are made familiar and comfortable with the basics as various competitive exams test you at the basic level. Also, 10+1 is the building block not only for 10+2 class but throughout his course of education & career . Students of all the boards (CBSE, ICSE and PSEB) are taught here. Provisions are made to cover the little difference in their subjects. The goal of the institute is to imbibe in students a love for learning commerce and to inculcate in them a desire to excel at every level. The Institute also aims at equipping the students with the intellectual and practical skills that are necessary to face the inevitable challenges in the future. Personal attention is given to each and every student. Regular group discussions and revision tests are conducted. Self-evaluation is also promoted. Weekly test is given and marks are conveyed to the Parent / Guardian.

10+2 COMMERCE

The class of 10+2 is the most important class for a commerce student. The marks scored in this class decide the future career of a student. The 12th class board marks are the gateway to the best commerce colleges of the region as well as the country. A student can study in an elite commerce college if he scores at least 96%. But, what if you don’t score 96%? A college is not the only option. There are various professional courses that a student can choose from such as CA, CS, CMA, CLAT etc. We offer coaching for professional courses as well. The students need to put in their maximum efforts and give their best performance. The reins of their future are in their own hands and The GK Institute provides proper guidance as to how to move them. We ensure that conceptual clarity is improved and students have proper understanding and deep knowledge. To score well in this class is a dream that we help in materializing. Quick, yet proper syllabus completion; regular revision, tests and doubt sessions; practical experience along with theoretical mastery; educative, competitive and a friendly environment: is what the institute provides. Weekly test is given and marks are conveyed to the Parent / Guardian.

CA FOUNDATION

These days many students are losing out interest in engineering & medical due to various reasons and are looking out for other options. CA-Foundation (CA-CPT) is definitely a buzzword in today’s world. Demand for certified accountants is sensed well in the industry. Students attracted towards CA-Foundation (CA-CPT) course tend to lead their career path as Chartered Accountants (CA) / Cost & Management Accountants (CMA) / Company Secretary (CS). Student’s interest in being an accounting professional is trending with good job openings in the market and handsome pay packets. In recent years, there is tremendous progress in scores achieved by the students in all India CA-Foundation (CA-CPT) exams. With high growth rate, students are looking for more accurate information and resources available near to them to know more about CA-Foundation curriculum. CA Foundation (CA-CPT) is an entry level examination leading to CA. A 12th Class pass student can take up this course.

CA INTER

CA Intermediate formerly called or popularly known as CA-IPCC is the Second level of the prestigious Chartered Accountancy Course which we know has 3 levels of rigorous examinations. The CA-Inter exam is taken after passing the 1st level exam i.e. CA-Foundation and nine months of study. As the level of competition and toughness of exam is much more in CA-Inter level, efforts of GK Faculty are also doubled for this level. With regular tests and complete conceptual clarity we prepare our students for all kind of questions in CA Examination. Our consistently improving results prove our efforts and level of dedication towards our students.
